4 /* Example 4: variant of examples 2 & 3, demonstrating I/O with split channels.
5  *
6  * Note that, for convenience of the demonstration, split-channel data is
7  * made available by deinterleaving data sourced from and sent to
8  * interleaved file-streams; this adds a lot of code to the example that,
9  * for purposes of understanding how to use split-channels, may safely be
10  * ignored. In a real application, the channel-data might never be
11  * interleaved; for example, the split-channel data output from the
12  * resampler might be sent directly to digital-to-analogue converters.
13  *
14  * Note also (not shown in the examples) that split/interleaved channels may
15  * be used for input and output independently.
16  */
