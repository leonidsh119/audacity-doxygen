\subsection{Detailed Description}
The \hyperlink{class_splash_dialog}{Splash\+Dialog} shows help information for Audacity when Audacity starts up. 

It was written for the benefit of N\+EW users who do not want to read the manual. The text of the dialog is kept short to increase the chance of it being read. The content is designed to reduce the most commonly asked questions about Audacity.
