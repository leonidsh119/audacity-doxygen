Mixes together all input tracks, applying any envelopes, amplitude gain, panning, and real-\/time effects in the process. 

Takes one or more tracks as input; of all the \hyperlink{class_wave_track}{Wave\+Track} s that are selected, it mixes them together, applying any envelopes, amplitude gain, panning, and real-\/time effects in the process. The resulting pair of tracks (stereo) are \char`\"{}rendered\char`\"{} and have no effects, gain, panning, or envelopes. Other sorts of tracks are ignored.
