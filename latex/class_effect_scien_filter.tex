\subsection{Detailed Description}
An \hyperlink{class_effect}{Effect} that applies \textquotesingle{}classical\textquotesingle{} I\+IR filters. 

Performs I\+IR filtering that emulates analog filters, specifically Butterworth, Chebyshev Type I and Type II. Highpass and lowpass filters are supported, as are filter orders from 1 to 10.
