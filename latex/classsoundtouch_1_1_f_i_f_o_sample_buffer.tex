\subsection{Detailed Description}
\hyperlink{struct_sample}{Sample} buffer working in F\+I\+FO (first-\/in-\/first-\/out) principle. The class takes care of storage size adjustment and data moving during input/output operations.

Notice that in case of stereo audio, one sample is considered to consist of both channel data.
